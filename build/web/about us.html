About Jin-Sung Service

Founded in 2021, Jin-Sung Service is a fast-growing Cameroonian eCommerce platform focused on delivering top-quality electronics, groceries, fashion, and home essentials across Cameroon and Central Africa.

We aim to connect communities through affordable and reliable digital commerce. Our team is passionate about service, innovation, and ensuring every customer experiences excellence.

Headquartered in Yaounde, Cameroon, Jin-Sung Service is committed to empowering local suppliers and leveraging mobile money to make shopping easy and secure for everyone.

Our Mission

To make high-quality products accessible to all Cameroonians through reliable delivery, mobile payment, and world-class support.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fast nationwide delivery
  • Secure payments via Orange & MTN Mobile Money
  • 24/7 customer service
  • Trusted by over 10,000 satisfied customers
